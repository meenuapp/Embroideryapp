Privacy Policy for Embroidery Business Suite

Last updated: [June 18, 2025]

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and managed when you use the Embroidery Business Suite mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password for the sole purpose of user authentication and account security. You may optionally provide a display name.
  • User-Generated Data: We store the data you create within the app, which includes tailor profiles and measurement data. This data is linked to your account to provide the core functionality of the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App's services.
  • Authenticate your access to your account.
  • Securely store and retrieve your tailor and measurement data so you can access it at any time.

3. Data Storage and Security

All your data, including account information and measurement data, is stored securely on Google's Firebase platform. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information or user-generated data with any third parties for any purpose. Your data is yours alone.

5. Data Retention

We will retain your information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal information and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
